The DossTones

We are a group of professionals..., including a couple of attorneys, a music teacher, a psychological counselor, a small-business owner..., and we have all been playing music in and around Nashville most of our lives. We play a lot of soul, jazz, blues and some pop oldies. The sax, B3/piano, and guitar give us range, and four of the players are also vocalists, so there is a lot of singing. We're flexible, dependable, and we enjoy playing early shows. Give us a shot..., we're confident you'll like what you hear!
